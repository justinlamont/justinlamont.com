LAMONT EGG NOG (tried & true)

Home Food Dranks
  • 2 quarts Half and Half
  • 1 quart heavy cream
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2.5 Tablespoons vanilla (madagascar is best for this recipe)
  • 1 dozen eggs
  • 1 cup bourbon
  • 1 cup rum (amber)
  • 1 cup confectioner's sugar

Separate eggs in 2 bowls. Place egg white in refrigerator for use later. In egg yoke bowl
