Corn’n Oil

placeholder description

  • 1 oz. dark rum (Standby uses Plantation)
  • 1.5 oz. falernum
  • .75 oz. fresh lime juice
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • 1 oz. black strap rum

Combine the dark rum, falernum and lime in a shaker tin with ice. Shake until chilled and strain into a Collins glass. Add crushed ice, then float the black strap and bitters over the top of the drink. Stir to combine before drinking.

Mai Tai

placeholder description

  • .75 Curaçao
  • 1 white rum
  • 2 dark rum
  • Half lime
  • 1/2 cup passion fruit nectar
  • .75 Licor 43
  • Mint

Chill tumblers in freezer. Ice machine cubes stored in bowl in freezer(six per drink). Two tumblers, one to put ingredients in and the other to pour back and forth four times. 3/4 shot glass Curaçao, one shot white Bacardi rum, two shots Meyers dark rum, juice half lime, 1/2 cup Passion Orange Nectar. Pour four time to mix. Place mix in chilled tumbler with six ice maker size cubes. In blender put 1/3 cup Passion Orange Nectar, 3/4 shot Licor 43. Slow pulse to mix then liquefy 30 sec. Roll two mint leaves between hands to bruise and place in iced mix and push down with fork. Layer foam from blender on top of drink(foam immediately before serving).
