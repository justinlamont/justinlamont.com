One Pot Pasta a la Lauren

Home Food Dranks
  • 16 ounces whole wheat spaghetti
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 package of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 large onion, thinly sliced
  • small container sliced black olives(approx 1 cup)
  • 1 package prosciutto
  • 12 leaves of fresh basil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced (I used 3 tablespoons of pre-minced garlic)
  • 1 tablespoon of oregano
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes (then add to taste at end)
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup whole milk ricotta
  • pecorino to taste
  • salt to taste at end

In a large skillet or flat pot, combine all ingredients except for the salt and cheese. Boil the mixture on high heat (without a cover) for 12-15 minutes. Make sure to stir the pasta every 5 minutes.
