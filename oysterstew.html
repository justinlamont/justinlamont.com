Dad's Baked Oysters

Home Food Dranks

Ingredients

  • Three to four dozen Blue Points shucked and reserve strained liquor and oysters
  • Half a pound of local bay scallops if available
  • 2 cups heavy cream plus two cups whole milk
  • 5-tablespoon flour
  • 12 tablespoons butter
  • Half cup minced parsley
  • About four slices of crisp bacon minced
  • 6-8 sautéed in olive oil garlic cloves sliced
For mirepoix
  • Sweet onion finely minced
  • Four celery stalks finely minced
  • One carrot finely minced

Make mirepoix then reserve. six slices of crusty bread browned in garlic oil in pan with some oregano, salt, pepper then food processor to crumbs 1-2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese Salt, Pepper, Cayenne pepper Use oil for bread and Mirepoix to sauté garlic slices and flavor oil Heat H cream and milk after making roux with flour and butter. Thicken and use wondra and more whole milk to titrate thickness. Sauté Mirepoix until very soft 20-25 min. When cream mixture is correct consistency add all other ingredients and heat until oysters and scallops are just cooked. Do not over cook. It bubbles lightly for several minutes. Serve with oyster crackers
