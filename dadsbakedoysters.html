Dad's Baked Oysters

Home Food Dranks
  • One half yellow onion finely minced
  • Juice one large lemon
  • One medium ripe tomato finely chopped
  • Four small garlic cloves minced with garlic press
  • Herb Garlic bread crumbs from Progresso (about half a cup) adjust to consistnecy
  • 1/2 tsp. Dijon Country Mustard
  • one tsp. Horseradish
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Salt ground (Not too much)
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • Thyme or Herb de Provence (about one tsp)
  • One stick of butter cut in quarters then half inch cubes (add after everything is well mixed)

Take about 20 large Blue Points. Wait to shuck until ready to put topping on and put in oven (Pre heat to 350) set oysters on BBQ grill rack. Make sure oysters are loose in shell. I found side entry shucking with hinge toward you with French style knife (sharp on one side and more narrow –pinch grip knife near tip when initially inserting blade to avoid injury, then twist blade, hold valves [shells] open and carefully and completely sever adductor from top [flat] shell) fun and fast. Make sure to swipe off grit and shell debris.

Mash and mix topping ingredients in glass bowl. Put about 1 tbsp. on top of each oyster on rack . Shave Parmesan on top and put in oven to bake for about 25 min. Remove and let cool for five minutes and serve with small fork on plates. Enjoy!
