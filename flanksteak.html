Flank Steak

Bring it out to room temp Season garlic parsley salt pepper glaze with Dijon mustard & 2 or three pats of butter. Place on tin foil & broil on low then high until corners of steak look crisp. Timing has to do with thickness. When you take it out bring sides of foil up to cover & let sit for 5 minutes or less. Total cooking usually 20-30 minutes max. FYI mustard is visible coating Remember to carve on an angle😀
